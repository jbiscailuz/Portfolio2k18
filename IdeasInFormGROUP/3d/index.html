About

Following the concept explored in the 2D Study, I decided to explore how technology affects natural forms. In reference to wearable tech, how does something foreign to our bodies affects us. How the object and our bodies itself react to the connection created. In order to represent this, I plucked a leaf from a plant and attached a piece of silk previously made in the 2D study. It transformed the leaf into a natural object to a surreal almost futuristic artifact. The evolution of the decay of the leaf and fabric will be further studied and documented.
